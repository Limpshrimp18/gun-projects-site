About Me

Hey, I'm Rocky — an engineer, gunsmith, and CNC machinist. I've spent years dialing in my craft to deliver precision gun work, custom modifications, and cutting-edge builds that balance function, durability, and a whole lot of style.

From slide cuts and optic mounts to stippling and frame mods, I handle everything with a perfectionist mindset. Whether I'm working with a Ghost Gunner, designing in Fusion 360, or testing new rail setups, I treat every project like it's the only one that matters.

This site is where I document the journey — every successful mod, every lesson learned, and every new idea that hits the bench.

Skills & Tools:

  • CNC Machining (Ghost Gunner, Coast Runner, custom setups)
  • Fusion 360 & CAM programming
  • Firearms customization & optic cuts
  • Stippling & frame work
  • Material testing (cerakote, polymer types)
